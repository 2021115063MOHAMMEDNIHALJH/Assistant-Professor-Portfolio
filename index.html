About

Hi! I am an Assistant Professor of Electronic Media at the St.Thomas College of Arts and Science, Affiliated to the University of Madras. My research focuses on the Role of New media in Environmental Security consciousness. I am pursuing my Ph.D. in Defence- Journalism at the University of Madras. I began as an undergrad in chemistry at the Manonmaniam Sundaranar University, Tamil Nadu, India. I completed my Post-graduations, Post-graduate diplomas, and Diplomas in various disciplines from various universities in Tamil Nadu India. My areas of study include Communication Research Methods, Film Language, Writing for media, and New Media. By fusing together insights from fields outside of Mass communication, I strive to deepen our understanding of how media shape the world.
